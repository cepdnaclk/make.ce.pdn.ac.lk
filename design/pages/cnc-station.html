Special Notes

  • Need to purchase the materials (except the spindle) required to build the CNC
  • Need a precious design of the CNC machine after the parts and the dimension confirmed
  • There need to have a small Desktop Computer or a Laptop to control the CNC machine
  • Need to design a small container to keep the tools and accessories of the CNC
  • UPS may be required if there no any backup power supply available
